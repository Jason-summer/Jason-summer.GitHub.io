#3 More

2020-04-13 4 min read # 小说

When Conner came about what happened to him, he couldn't feel more in shame than he had been now. How stupid, how foolish, how ridiculous he was, that's what he couldn't stop thinking. When he calmed down, he started wondering. He had lost face, now he must act more like a common people. Thought that, he made his determine.

He put down the cup after he finished his, what he gave him's coffee. Do not forget the business, he reminded himself.

"So, what are the people here live for? Fishing I guess?" asked by Conner.

"Touring and leather making actually." answered by Joe, who didn't mind the rude of his guest.

Realizing his rudeness, Conner tried to pretend gentler.

"So, what's meaning of the clothing like that?" Conner pointed at the clothes he was wearing. A suit of leather... That made him remember that kind of gay he had seen in England.

"Those are our belief. A belief of male, vigorous(雄浑的), strength and so." Joe tried to complain in relief as he knew Conner might not understand now.

Silence, again. Conner thought there's no more embarrassing situation for past and future. Luckily, Conner himself broke it. "eh...Would you like to show me around? The island."

Joe raised eyebrows then said, "Of course, if you wanted." He stood up and led the way.

It's a awesome island. There's a population of no more than a million but all men. Have a well-deserved reputation of gay island. German is its official language but most people can speak English quite well. There's some bars and a modern hospital that is guarded well. And it has a school!

How can they get students for it? Conner was wondering when walking along the street with Joe. Joe glanced at his face, it seemed like Conner had forgotten what just happened. Thank god. Joe said in his mind. How dare he so bold(大胆的) to do those things. He was a bit regretful. "I must leave a bad image(形象)."

It was a time when Conner saw a person whose clothes wasn't made with leather. He asked Joe why. "I thought people here can't live without leather." He threw a taunt(嘲讽).

"Well, sorry the island is not so bad as you may think." Joe shrugged(耸肩). "We are not always in leather. Sometimes we dress like sunny boys. But I'm sure there's not a clothing like arrogant(傲慢的) businessmen."

"Here we go." He dragged Conner into a clothing shop. When they came out, with he shaved, Conner looked like a young boy although he was in his 30s. He now wore in white T-shirt, coffee-coloured overalls(工装裤) and white socks in Adidas sports shoes. A white skirt and a suspenders were in shopping bag while his original(原先的) clothing in another one.

"Not bad." Stood in front of the mirror.

"You will love here, as I said."
